Saasfy is a modern SaaS template that helps you build a beautiful and responsive website for your
SaaS business. It includes a variety of components and features that you can use to create a
stunning website.

## Features

Saasfy comes with a variety of features that make it easy to build a website for your SaaS business.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Authentication" icon="lock">
    Saasfy comes with a complete authentication system that includes login, registration, and
    password reset pages.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Pricing" icon="dollar-sign">
    You can easily create pricing tables and pricing cards to showcase your pricing plans.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Billing" icon="credit-card">
    Saasfy integrates with Stripe to provide a seamless billing experience for your customers.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Workspaces" icon="grid">
    The workspaces feature allows users to create multiple workspaces and switch between them
    easily.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Settings" icon="gear">
    The settings page allows users to update their profile information and change their password.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Domain Management" icon="globe">
    Users can add custom domains to their workspaces and manage them from the dashboard.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
